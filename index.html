What does it mean to swim with stingray?

Our reef experts will take you on a tour of the best locations to spot beautiful sea creatures and coral reefs. All the stingray you will meet on your tour are safe to touch and have had their stingers harmlessly clipped.

You can choose between three fun water activities. You can casually go snorkeling in the shallow reefs or take an underwater walk where you can comfortably breath while walking the ocean floor or even take a bike ride through the coral reefs and be one with the sea.

Go Snorkeling

Explore the best locations to touch and hold stingray. You can glance around and see the world as the fish do.
